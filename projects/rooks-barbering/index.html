FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers to help you feel confident before your visit.

Is this actual therapy?

Not quite! While I can't replace a therapist, you'll definitely leave feeling uplifted and rejuvenated. My goal is to provide a space where you can unwind and share your thoughts while getting a fresh cut.

What if I don't know?

No worries! That’s exactly why I’m here—to guide you through the process. Together, we’ll find a style that suits you perfectly.

Can I just chat?

I love a good conversation, but the haircut is a must! Think of it as a package deal—great cuts and even better chats. You’ll leave feeling fabulous and heard.

How do I book?

Booking is easy! You can secure your spot online or reach out via call or text. Just remember, my schedule fills up quickly, so don’t wait too long!

What if I'm late?

Life happens, I get it! Just let me know if you're running behind. I’ll do my best to accommodate you, but please try to arrive on time for the full experience.
