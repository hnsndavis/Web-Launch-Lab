FAQs

Find answers to your most common questions about our LED screen rentals.

What is SpiritLED?

SpiritLED is a mobile LED screen rental company. We specialize in providing high-quality screens for various events. Our services include delivery, setup, and operation.

How do I book?

Booking is simple! Just share your event date and location with us. We’ll confirm availability and provide a detailed quote.

What sizes are available?

Our primary offering is a 23×13 ft hydraulic LED screen. This size is perfect for large audiences. We can also discuss custom solutions based on your needs.

What’s included in rental?

Our rental includes the LED screen, power, audio equipment, and on-site operation. We handle all technical aspects to ensure a smooth experience. You can focus on your guests while we manage the visuals.

Do you offer support?

Yes, we provide full technical support during your event. Our experienced team is there to ensure everything runs seamlessly. You can trust us to handle any issues that arise.
